# Comprehensive 60-Question Ontario Will Questionnaire

## SECTION 1: TESTATOR IDENTIFICATION & CAPACITY (Questions 1-8)

1. **Full Legal Name** - What is your complete legal name (including any maiden names or aliases)?
2. **Date of Birth** - What is your date of birth?
3. **Current Address** - What is your current residential address?
4. **Marital Status** - What is your current marital status (Single/Married/Common-law/Divorced/Widowed)?
5. **Spouse Information** - If married/common-law, what is your spouse's full legal name and date of birth?
6. **Previous Marriages** - Have you been married or in a common-law relationship before? If yes, provide details.
7. **Mental Capacity Confirmation** - Do you confirm that you are of sound mind and understand the nature and consequences of making a will?
8. **Testamentary Intent** - Do you confirm that this will expresses your true wishes and intentions?

## SECTION 2: FAMILY STRUCTURE (Questions 9-18)

9. **Children** - Do you have any children (biological, adopted, or step-children)? If yes, list names, dates of birth, and whether adopted.
10. **Grandchildren** - Do you have any grandchildren? If yes, list names and dates of birth.
11. **Dependents** - Are there any other individuals who depend on you financially? If yes, provide details.
12. **Estranged Family** - Are there any family members you intentionally wish to exclude from your will? If yes, provide names and relationship.
13. **Blended Family** - Do you have a blended family situation (previous spouse's children, etc.)? Provide details.
14. **Adult Children Status** - Are all your children adults (18+)? If not, provide ages.
15. **Children's Relationships** - Do any of your children have special circumstances (disabilities, substance issues, financial irresponsibility)?
16. **Grandchildren Guardianship** - If you have minor grandchildren, should they be included in guardianship provisions?
17. **Family Conflict** - Are there any known family conflicts or tensions that might affect inheritance?
18. **Unmarried Partner** - If you have an unmarried partner, do you wish to include them in your will?

## SECTION 3: REAL PROPERTY (Questions 19-25)

19. **Primary Residence** - Do you own your primary residence? If yes, provide address and estimated value.
20. **Mortgage Status** - Is there a mortgage on your primary residence? If yes, provide lender and approximate balance.
21. **Investment Properties** - Do you own any investment properties (rental, commercial)? If yes, list addresses and values.
22. **Vacation Properties** - Do you own any vacation or cottage properties? If yes, provide details.
23. **Land Holdings** - Do you own any land or vacant properties? If yes, provide details.
24. **Joint Ownership** - Is any real property owned jointly with others? If yes, specify which properties and co-owners.
25. **Property Disposition** - For each property, who should inherit it? Should it be sold or retained?

## SECTION 4: FINANCIAL ASSETS (Questions 26-35)

26. **Bank Accounts** - List all bank accounts (institution, account type, approximate balance).
27. **Investment Accounts** - Do you have investment accounts (stocks, bonds, mutual funds)? If yes, list institutions and approximate values.
28. **RRSPs** - Do you have registered retirement savings plans? If yes, list institutions and approximate values.
29. **TFSAs** - Do you have tax-free savings accounts? If yes, list institutions and approximate values.
30. **GICs & Fixed Income** - Do you have GICs or other fixed income investments? If yes, provide details.
31. **Pensions** - Do you receive or are entitled to any pensions (employer, government)? If yes, provide details.
32. **Life Insurance** - Do you have life insurance policies? If yes, list face values and current beneficiary designations.
33. **Annuities** - Do you have any annuities? If yes, provide details.
34. **Safe Deposit Box** - Do you have a safe deposit box? If yes, where and what does it contain?
35. **Financial Account Beneficiaries** - Which financial accounts have designated beneficiaries that pass outside the will?

## SECTION 5: BUSINESS INTERESTS (Questions 36-40)

36. **Business Ownership** - Do you own or have an interest in any business? If yes, describe the business and your ownership percentage.
37. **Business Type** - What type of business is it (sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation)?
38. **Business Value** - What is the estimated value of your business interest?
39. **Buy-Sell Agreement** - Is there a buy-sell agreement in place? If yes, provide details.
40. **Business Succession** - Who should inherit your business interest or what should happen to it?

## SECTION 6: DIGITAL & CRYPTOCURRENCY ASSETS (Questions 41-44)

41. **Cryptocurrency** - Do you own any cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)? If yes, list types and approximate values.
42. **Digital Accounts** - Do you have digital accounts (PayPal, online banking, etc.)? If yes, provide details.
43. **Online Business** - Do you own any online businesses, websites, or domains? If yes, provide details.
44. **Digital Subscriptions** - Do you have valuable digital subscriptions or accounts? If yes, list them.

## SECTION 7: PERSONAL PROPERTY (Questions 45-50)

45. **Vehicles** - Do you own any vehicles? If yes, list make, model, and estimated value.
46. **Jewelry & Collectibles** - Do you own jewelry, art, or collectibles? If yes, provide details and estimated values.
47. **Household Furnishings** - Do you have household furnishings of significant value? If yes, describe.
48. **Sentimental Items** - Are there specific personal items you wish to leave to specific people?
49. **Tools & Equipment** - Do you own tools, equipment, or machinery of value? If yes, provide details.
50. **Pets** - Do you have any pets? If yes, who should care for them?

## SECTION 8: DEBTS & LIABILITIES (Questions 51-54)

51. **Mortgages** - List all mortgages (lender, property, approximate balance).
52. **Personal Loans** - Do you have any personal loans? If yes, provide details.
53. **Credit Card Debts** - Do you have credit card debts? If yes, approximate total.
54. **Other Debts** - Do you have any other debts (business loans, tax liabilities, etc.)?

## SECTION 9: EXECUTOR & TRUSTEE SELECTION (Questions 55-58)

55. **Primary Executor** - Who should be your executor (estate trustee)? Provide full name, address, and relationship.
56. **Alternate Executor** - Who should be your alternate executor if the primary cannot serve?
57. **Executor Compensation** - Should your executor receive compensation? If yes, how much or what percentage?
58. **Executor Powers** - Should your executor have broad powers or limited powers? Specify any restrictions.

## SECTION 10: GUARDIANSHIP & TRUSTS (Questions 59-60)

59. **Guardians for Minor Children** - If you have minor children, who should be their guardian? Provide names and addresses for both personal guardian and property guardian.
60. **Special Trusts** - Do you want to create any special trusts (for disabled children, spendthrift beneficiaries, Henson trusts)? If yes, provide details.

## CONDITIONAL FOLLOW-UP QUESTIONS (Based on Answers)

### If testator has minor children:
- Q61: At what age should children receive their inheritance (18, 21, 25)?
- Q62: Should funds be held in trust until that age?
- Q63: Who should manage the funds until distribution?

### If testator has significant assets:
- Q64: Are you interested in probate fee minimization strategies?
- Q65: Should any assets be placed in trust during lifetime?
- Q66: Should any assets be gifted during your lifetime?

### If testator has business interests:
- Q67: Should the business be sold or continued by heirs?
- Q68: Are there key employees who should have first right of purchase?
- Q69: Should business debts be paid from estate or business assets?

### If testator has disabled beneficiaries:
- Q70: Should a Henson Trust be created to preserve government benefits?
- Q71: Who should manage the trust?
- Q72: What are the trustee's instructions regarding distributions?

### If testator has blended family:
- Q73: Should biological children be treated differently than step-children?
- Q74: Should spouse receive all assets or share with children?
- Q75: Are there any specific provisions for ex-spouse's children?

### If testator has significant digital assets:
- Q76: Who should have access to digital accounts after your death?
- Q77: Should digital assets be liquidated or transferred?
- Q78: Are there specific instructions for social media accounts?

## DOCUMENT GENERATION REQUIREMENTS

### Will Opening Clause
"I, [Full Name], of [Address], being of sound mind and memory, do hereby revoke all former wills, codicils, and testamentary dispositions made by me and declare this to be my last Will and Testament."

### Will Family Clause
"I declare that I am [marital status] and that [spouse/no spouse information]. I have [number] children: [names]. I have [number] grandchildren: [names]."

### Will Executor Clause
"I hereby appoint [Executor Name] of [Address] to be my executor and trustee. If [Executor Name] is unable or unwilling to act, I appoint [Alternate Executor Name] as my executor and trustee."

### Will Specific Bequests
"I give, devise, and bequeath to [Beneficiary Name] of [Address] [specific item or amount]."

### Will Residual Estate Clause
"I give, devise, and bequeath the remainder of my estate to [beneficiaries] in the following proportions: [percentages]."

### Will Trust Provisions
"I direct my executor to hold [assets] in trust for [beneficiary] with the following terms: [trust terms]."

### Will Execution Clause
"IN WITNESS WHEREOF I have hereunto set my hand to this my Will this [date].

SIGNED, PUBLISHED AND DECLARED by the above-named [Testator Name] as their last Will and Testament in the presence of us both, present at the same time, who at their request and in their presence and in the presence of each other have hereunto subscribed our names as witnesses."
